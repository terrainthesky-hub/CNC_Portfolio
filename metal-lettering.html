Metal Lettering Experiments

Fabricating custom letter shapes from metal stock using manual tools.

About The Project

This project documents my process of learning and refining manual fabrication techniques. Starting with basic cuts and progressing to more complex shapes, I experimented with various tools including bandsaws, jigsaws, and dremels to create custom letter designs from raw stock. It was a fantastic exercise in precision, problem-solving, and embracing aesthetic choices, like leaving the dye on the final piece for a weathered, industrial look.
