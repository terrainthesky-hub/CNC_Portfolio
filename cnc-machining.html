CNC & Manual Machining

From digital designs to physical objects using CAM and traditional milling.

About The Project

This collection highlights projects created with both an automated Genmitsu CNC router and a traditional manual mill. It demonstrates the full workflow from CAD design and CAM toolpathing in Fusion 360 to a finished product. A key takeaway from the CNC work was learning to troubleshoot real-world issues, like securing stock properly and recovering a job after a runtime error. The manual mill projects emphasize the precision and hands-on skill required for foundational machining.
